General Municipal Ordinance - Schedule Roleplay

Welcome to Schedule Roleplay

This document contains the official rules and regulations for the Schedule Roleplay community. All players are expected to read and follow these guidelines to ensure a positive and enjoyable experience for everyone.

Legal-Technical Information

Notes Regarding This Ordinance

Important Notice

  • All rules apply to all community members without exception
  • Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse for violations
  • Rules may be updated periodically - stay informed
  • Severe violations may result in permanent bans

General Provisions

A1 - Scope

Rule Scope

  1. These rules apply to all players within the Schedule Roleplay server environment.
  2. The rules are in effect 24/7 and apply to all areas of the server.
  3. Community Discord and other official platforms are also subject to these rules.

A2 - Validity

Rule Validity

  1. These rules are effective immediately upon publication.
  2. Previous versions of rules are superseded by this document.
  3. Updates will be announced through official channels.

A3 - Penalties

Punishment System

  1. Violations are categorized into 5 categories of increasing severity.
  2. Punishments may include warns, kicks, temporary bans, or permanent bans.
  3. Repeat offenders face escalated consequences.
  4. Appeals can be submitted through the proper channels.

A4 - Additional Laws and Rules

Additional Guidelines

  1. Server staff may implement additional temporary rules as needed.
  2. Common sense and respect should guide all interactions.
  3. When in doubt, ask a staff member for clarification.

Violations - Public Order Disruption (RP)

Article 1 - FailRP & Powergaming

FailRP & Powergaming - Category 1-5

  1. Players must always act realistically within their character.
  2. Powergaming (forcing actions on others) is strictly prohibited.
  3. Character knowledge and real-world knowledge must be separated.
  4. Death RP must be taken seriously - no immediate return to conflict.
  5. Unrealistic actions that break immersion are not allowed.

Article 2 - Cheats

Cheating - Category 5 (Permanent Ban)

  1. Use of any third-party software to gain unfair advantages is prohibited.
  2. This includes but is not limited to: aimbots, wallhacks, speed hacks, money cheats.
  3. First offense results in immediate permanent ban.

Article 3 - Exploits

Exploiting - Category 2-5

  1. Exploiting game bugs or glitches for personal gain is prohibited.
  2. Report bugs to staff instead of exploiting them.
  3. Severity of punishment depends on the exploit used and damage caused.

Article 4 - RP Disruption

Roleplay Disruption - Category 1-3

  1. Intentionally disrupting others' roleplay experiences is prohibited.
  2. This includes trolling, griefing, and non-serious RP behavior.
  3. Random killing (RDM) and vehicle destruction (VDM) are forms of disruption.

Article 5 - Metagaming

Metagaming - Category 2-4

  1. Using out-of-character information in-character is strictly prohibited.
  2. This includes information from Discord, streams, or other external sources.
  3. Player names, locations, and activities learned OOC cannot be used IC.

Article 6 - Professional Clothing

Professional Attire - Category 1-2

  1. Players in professional roles must wear appropriate clothing.
  2. Emergency services, government officials, and business employees included.
  3. Failure to maintain professional appearance may result in job loss.
